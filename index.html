Play Store

Features

Lookup

This app allows you to search words within entries. Just start typing with Gurmukhi, for that you can install Google Indic Keyboard.

Volume Buttons Navigation

When viewing articles, volume up/down buttons scroll article content or, if at the bottom (top) of the page, move to the next (previous) article. Long press scrolls all the way to the bottom (top).

Bookmarks and History

Visited articles are automatically added to history and appear in History tab. Articles can also be bookmarked (tap Bookmark icon when viewing article). Bookmarked articles appear in Bookmarks tab. Bookmarks and history can be filtered and sorted by time or article title.

Random Article

Tapping application logo in main activity finds a random word in Mahan Kosh and opens corresponding articles.

Night Mode

Best styling is used to make this app usable at night. Just go to setting and change mode to dark.
